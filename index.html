How Main Campaign Sync Works

Step #1

Facebook user fills out your Facebook ads instant lead form

Step #2

Main Campaign automatically syncs inbound Facebook leads to sales CRM

Step #3

Lead makes a purchase in an offline setting (example; In person)

Step #4

Sales team updates sales CRM with the Leads purchase revenue

Step #5

Main Campaign attributes Leads purchase revenue to Facebook marketing campaigns. Now you know exactly which campaigns are driving purchases for your business

Understand how many sales are driven from your campaigns

The issue with tracking marketing data in an online setting is that youre not getting the full picture. Although youre still making sales offline. its nearly impossible to track exactly what online campaigns are doin the job, and which ones arent...until now.

With Main Campaign, you can integrate your conversion data with Facebook conversion API and Offline Conversion Tracking directly.
